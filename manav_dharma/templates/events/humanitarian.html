Humanity Matters for Us

Humanity is the best policy

    Manav Dharma is an organization where people from far and wide have recognized and experienced the eternal life energy by knowing the knowledge of soul through spiritual education and have committed themselves as volunteers to aware humans to awaken the dormant energy through experiencing the real self within. With the blessings of Sadgurudev Shri Satpal Ji Maharaj the organization has been bringing about drastic changes in the society and flourishing day by day. The organization is moving ahead with the great goal of disseminating spiritual knowledge throughout the world to motivate human first know thyself and develop brotherhood and humanity in every human heart and soul.

Humanity Comes First

Manav Dharma priority is to uplift humanity and spread the message of universal brotherhood around the globe..Manav Dharma inspire all to feel the beauty of human life and it teaches the people the art of living and understand human potential.

Manav Dharma is for all races..Mnav Dharma believe that before we became hindu,muslim,christain,sikh we were human beings so our real religion is Humanity above humanity nothing comes.

We have been conducting various spiritual seminar,Santa samagam ,blood donation camp, cleaning campaign ,Mission Education and other activities to uplift humanity and make people aware of being human and we also develop the feeling of Patriotism.
